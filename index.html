Easy volunteering that creates real connection

We are building a portal that pairs people into two‑person teams for 2-5 hours local micro‑tasks. It’s a low‑commitment onramp for newcomers and a powerful way for NGOs to recruit volunteers who won’t sign up for long shifts. For NGOs & city offices: recruit newcomers without long commitments

Micro‑task funnel

Publish 2-5 hour tasks with clear start/end. We handle duo‑matching, reminders, and attendance tracking.

Conversion to regular roles

After 2–3 micro‑tasks, interested duos get a nudge to try a longer shift or join a project—on the NGO’s terms.

Pilot goals & expected impact

Pilot in Göttingen, 6 months, targeting 2,000 users and 1,000 duo sessions. Primary outcome: reduced loneliness (UCLA‑3) at 30/90 days. Secondary: NGO conversion to longer roles, repeat‑pairing, stable‑tie formation, volunteer hours.

FAQ

Is this a dating app?

No. It’s a volunteering platform engineered for one‑to‑one connection. Some duos will become friends or start dating—through shared purpose, not swiping.

Can NGOs use this to recruit volunteers?

Yes. The platform is designed as an onramp for people who avoid long commitments. Micro‑tasks + bonding time → higher conversion to regular roles.

What does the voucher cover?

A simple meal/snack and drink. We aim for partner‑co‑funded vouchers so the average subsidy stays low (≈ €6 per duo in the pilot).

How do you measure impact?

UCLA‑3 loneliness scale at baseline/30/90 days; NGO conversion; re‑pairing and stable‑tie formation; volunteer hours.
